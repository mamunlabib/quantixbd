Who We Are

Our Story

Quantix BD was founded by four graduates of the University of Dhaka who spent years building careers in fast-paced professional environments before deciding to come together and build something of their own.

After working with international teams and learning how modern commerce really runs, we set out to start an import commerce business with a simple mission — to source genuinely useful, high-quality products from around the world and deliver them at honest, affordable prices to homes across Bangladesh.

We believe shopping should be easy, trustworthy, and friendly. Every product we offer is hand-picked, every order is treated personally, and every customer is treated like family.

Who We Are

Our Story

It started with frustration.

Like most Bangladeshis, we were tired of paying too much for products that didn't last, or simply not being able to find the innovative, useful things we saw people enjoying around the world.

We were four friends — university graduates who had spent years working in professional, international environments. We understood quality. We understood how global commerce works. And we saw a clear gap — Bangladesh deserved better access to genuinely good products, at prices that made sense.

So in 2026, we decided to stop waiting for someone else to fix it.

Quantix BD was born with a simple but powerful promise — to personally handpick useful, high-quality products from around the world and bring them directly to your doorstep in Bangladesh. No middlemen. No compromise on quality. No confusion about who you're buying from.

We're not a faceless online store. We're four real people who put our names, faces, and reputations behind every single product we sell. When you order from Quantix BD, you're not just buying a product — you're buying from people who genuinely care.
